Privacy Policy for NoterLight

NoterLight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity.

All notes and highlights you create are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome storage.local API. This data is never transmitted to any external server or third-party service.

The extension does not use cookies, trackers, analytics, or any kind of background telemetry.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at your.email@example.com.
